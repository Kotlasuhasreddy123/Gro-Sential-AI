Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026
Summary: We collect only what we need to run the app. We don't sell your data. You can delete your account and all data at any time.

1. Who We Are

Gro-Sential AI ("we", "us", "our") is an AI-powered food waste reduction platform operated by Suhas Reddy Kotla. Contact: suhasreddykotla222@gmail.com

2. What Data We Collect

  • Account data: Email address, username, password (hashed with bcrypt — never stored in plain text)
  • Location data: ZIP/postal code only — used to find nearby food trading partners. We never collect GPS coordinates.
  • Food inventory data: Item names, quantities, expiry dates you enter or scan
  • Images: Photos you upload for food scanning are processed by AWS Rekognition and immediately discarded — we do not store your images
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, device type — collected via Google Analytics (anonymized)

3. How We Use AI

AI Consent Notice: Gro-Sential AI uses artificial intelligence services including:
  • AWS Rekognition — to identify food items in photos you upload
  • AWS Bedrock (Claude 3 Haiku by Anthropic) — to generate personalized recipes, expiry alerts, and chatbot responses

AI-generated content is for informational purposes only and is not a substitute for professional food safety advice. AI suggestions may occasionally be inaccurate.

4. How We Share Data

  • AWS (Amazon Web Services): Cloud infrastructure, AI processing, database storage
  • Google Analytics: Anonymized usage statistics
  • Other users: Only your username and ZIP code are visible to other users in the trading system — never your email or full address
  • We never sell your data to third parties

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can request deletion at any time by emailing us. We will delete all your data within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

  • Access your data — request a copy at any time
  • Correct your data — update via account settings
  • Delete your data — email us or use the delete account option
  • Opt out of analytics — use browser Do Not Track or an ad blocker

7. Cookies

We use session cookies for login and Google Analytics cookies for usage tracking. No advertising cookies are used.

8. Children's Privacy

Gro-Sential AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We will notify registered users by email of any material changes to this policy.
